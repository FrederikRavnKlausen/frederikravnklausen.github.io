Hi! I'm a postdoc at Princeton University in mathematical physics mentored by Michael Aizenman. Below you will find an overview of my research interest as well as a list of publications.

Research Interests

I'm broadly interested in mathematical physics, probability and quantum information and recently also elections. I use classical and quantum stochastic geometric methods to attack the Ising model, Anderson localization, open quantum systems etc. You are very welcome to reach out!
