Hi! I'm a postdoc at Princeton University in mathematical physics mentored by Michael Aizenman.

Before that I was in QMATH and NQCP at the University of Copenhagen.

Below you will find an overview of my research interest as well as a list of publications.

Research Interests

I'm broadly interested in mathematical physics, probability and quantum information and recently also elections. Often, I use classical and quantum stochastic geometric methods to investigate various problems concerning the Ising model, Anderson localization, open quantum systems etc.

You are very welcome to reach out! I have a long list of things that I'd like to know - some of them are on Mathoverflow.
